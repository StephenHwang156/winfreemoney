What You Should Order At Starbucks



What is one coffee store that we see just as often as Tim Hortons in Canada?


The answer is Starbucks!


Starbucks offers comfortable and cozy environment, along with nice decorations that attracts people into checking out the store.


Well, this article will give you some recommendations to what you should get when you visit the store. If you like the flavour of matcha/greentea and you are craving for something cold and refreshing, I recommend the Greentea Frappuccino. It is cold, refreshing, and delicious. The greentea flavour adds a bit of bitterness that evens out the sweetness so the drink is not too sweet. It is topped off with some freshly whipped cream. Although I do recommend cutting the syrup by 1/5 for optimal sweetness.

However, if you are not a fan of their Frappuccino, I have another recommendation, their Mango Dragonfruit Refresher with Lemonade. It is abosultely refreshing and light which is good if you are not a fan of the heavy whipped cream and iced mixture. This is a perfect drink for any day.


Here are my recommendations and I hope you will enjoy it!!!


Note: Both drinks contain some caffeine so think about it before purchasing it. Although I think they are good subsitutes for coffee when you want some caffeine but not too much that you start feeling weird from it.
